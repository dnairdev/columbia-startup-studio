# Interview 05: Durval Pereira

**Role/Title:** Head Coach
**Organization:** Cheshire United

---

## 1) Context

**Q: What is your role?**
A: I coach youth players and help them navigate the college recruiting process, especially players who don't fully understand how U.S. recruiting works.

## 2) Recruiting Workflow

**Q: How do your players contact coaches?**
A: Mostly email and tournament exposure. I help them prepare highlight videos and write emails to programs.

## 3) Tools Used

**Q: Tools used?**
A: YouTube highlights, email, WhatsApp, and sometimes recruiting services.

## 4) Pain Points

**Q: Biggest issue players face?**
A: Players don't know which schools are realistic for them and often email hundreds of coaches without responses.

**Q: What fails most often?**
A: Follow-up communication. Players send one email and never follow up or they lose track of who responded.

**Q: What would help them most?**
A: Guidance and organization, knowing which coaches viewed their film and reminders to follow up.

## 5) Reaction to Platform Idea

**Q: Would a single recruiting platform help?**
A: Yes. Especially for international players. Many talented players don't get recruited simply because they don't understand the process or don't reach the right coaches.
