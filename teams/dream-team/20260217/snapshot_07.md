## Overall Signal

🟡 **YELLOW** — Mixed signal. Pain is real (film editing grind, mass outreach tedium) but he's already a power user of multiple platforms and his success came from an ID camp, not platform-based recruiting. Unclear if a new tool adds enough value to switch.

## One-Sentence Takeaway

Power users of existing recruiting tools still find the process exhausting, but they're skeptical a new platform can beat what they already have — the differentiator needs to be clear and immediate.
