# Interview 08: Jackson Ten Over

**Role/Title:** Defender
**Organization:** Columbia Men's Soccer (NCAA Division I)

---

## 1) Context

**Q: Sport/position and situation?**
A: Defender for Columbia University, recruited through club team coach. Sent out emails but didn't get much response.

## 2) Recruiting Struggles

**Q: Hardest part about recruiting?**
A: Getting a coach to really take interest in him after sending out information and film.

## 3) Organization

**Q: How did you organize coaches?**
A: Just kept the coaches' contacts through email and text, had a spreadsheet for schools in general.

**Q: Problems?**
A: Sometimes forgot some of the coaches he reached out to. The main problem was just not getting the feedback from coaches. A lot of cold emails and hard getting initial interest from coaches.

## 4) Film Tools

**Q: Tools used?**
A: Used a service where you would submit film and they would create the highlight tape and post it on YouTube to send that link to coaches in emails.

**Q: Issue with film?**
A: He didn't feel like the film really showed his best attributes. A lot of coaches wanted full game films.

## 5) Concept Reaction

**Q: Would one recruiting app help?**
A: Yes, keeping everything organized and also would help contact coaches with all my info in one profile.
