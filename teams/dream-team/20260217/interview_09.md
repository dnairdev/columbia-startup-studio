# Interview 09: Kevin Wehner

**Role/Title:** Assistant Director of Basketball Communications
**Organization:** St. John's Men's Basketball

---

## Context

"I handle a lot of the digital content and recruiting visuals."
"I'm around everything — visits, edits, social, creative direction."
"Brand matters more than people think in recruiting."

## Recruiting

"Discovery still starts at AAU and referrals, but social plays a role."
"If a kid's film looks clean and intentional, that stands out immediately."
"Presentation is part of evaluation, whether we admit it or not."

## Tools Used

Still Hudl for film during recruiting. Also get sent film directly from HS programs. Twitter and Instagram for presence — how they present themselves digitally.
"Everything lives in different places. That's the annoying part."

## How to Improve

"I wish we had better context with film. Who's guarding him? What level?"
"I'd love a way to compare clips side by side."
"Right now it's tabs open everywhere."

## On Our Idea

"If it allows finding under-scouted recruits with real organization, that's interesting."
"It can't just be another highlight feed. It has to add structure."
"If it helps us tell our story (reach recruits) and evaluate cleaner, I'd use it."

*Willing to help however he can in prototype stage.*
