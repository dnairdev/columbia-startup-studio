# Interview 07: Cy Sokolowski

**Role/Title:** Striker
**Organization:** Columbia Men's Soccer (NCAA Division I). Semi-pro USL 2 experience.

---

## 1) Context

**Q: Sport/position and situation?**
A: Striker for Columbia University, recruited through club team film and sending emails to coaches.

## 2) Recruiting Struggles

**Q: Hardest part about recruiting?**
A: The hardest part was gathering all the film, as that was the only way of exposure living in Denver and going after schools mainly in the northeast. Going through hours and hours of film. Sending out all the emails to so many coaches, keeping up with all the coaches and keeping in contact.

## 3) Organization

**Q: How did you organize coaches?**
A: Created spreadsheets on his own. Used NCSA, SportsRecruits, ConnectSoccer. This software just gives access to databases and emails.

**Q: Problems?**
A: The apps would help him reach out and remember what coaches he emailed, but was very tedious as he is putting in all that effort on his own. Many many coaches, won't stand out where he is from.

## 4) Film Tools

**Q: Tools used?**
A: Highlights from the club on Hudl, clip everything on Hudl, and then exported every individual clip on Hudl to make reels on iMovie, put on YouTube and have as pinned video on recruits profile on SportsRecruits.

**Q: Issue with film?**
A: Doing the tedious work all by himself mixing the clips for a couple reels.

## 5) Concept Reaction

**Q: Would one recruiting app help?**
A: Maybe. Biggest problem was that the app he was using had a lot of good features. Ultimately sending multiple emails got him an offer after going to an ID camp.
