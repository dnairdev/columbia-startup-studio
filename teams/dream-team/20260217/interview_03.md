# Interview 03: Brandon Newman

**Role/Title:** Center Midfielder
**Organization:** Cal Poly San Luis Obispo Men's Soccer (NCAA Division I)

---

## 1) Context

**Q: Sport/position and situation?**
A: I'm a center midfielder at Cal Poly SLO. I was recruited mainly through club soccer and national showcases.

## 2) Recruiting Struggles

**Q: Hardest part about recruiting?**
A: Visibility. There are so many players emailing coaches that you don't know if your video was even opened.

**Q: Biggest frustration?**
A: You send film but never know if a coach watched it.

## 3) Organization

**Q: How did you organize coaches?**
A: Notes app and email folders.

**Q: Problems?**
A: Conversations happen everywhere — email, text, and sometimes Instagram. It's hard to track who is actually interested.

## 4) Film Tools

**Q: Tools used?**
A: Hudl and YouTube for highlights.

**Q: Issue with film?**
A: Coaches sometimes ask for full games after and you have to go back and send everything again.

## 5) Concept Reaction

**Q: Would one recruiting app help?**
A: Yes, especially if you could see whether coaches viewed your video and track interest level.
