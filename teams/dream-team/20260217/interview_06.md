# Interview 06: Ryan Yang

**Role/Title:** Center Midfielder / Winger
**Organization:** Columbia Men's Soccer (NCAA Division I)

---

## 1) Context

**Q: Sport/position and situation?**
A: I'm mainly a winger here at Columbia, recruited through high school and club soccer, various showcases and tournaments.

## 2) Recruiting Struggles

**Q: Hardest part about recruiting?**
A: Finding schools that would support international students financially. A lot of initial interest from coaches though, that was not a problem.

## 3) Organization

**Q: How did you organize coaches?**
A: Didn't organize — remembered through email and text threads. Physical notebook where he would take down notes about schools and calls.

**Q: Problems?**
A: Hard to track over emails and messages what coaches were who, needed to record on physical notes.

## 4) Film Tools

**Q: Tools used?**
A: Highlights from the club, making highlight film myself.

**Q: Issue with film?**
A: A lot of the video quality wasn't great, tedious to go through full game to find clips and also send full film to coaches.

## 5) Concept Reaction

**Q: Would one recruiting app help?**
A: Maybe, if you can see what schools are with what coaches and advantages of schools like financial help.
