# Interview 04: Jordi Blanco

**Role/Title:** Director / Scout
**Organization:** Serrano FC

---

## 1) Context

**Q: What is your role?**
A: I am a club director and scout. My goal is to help players progress and also identify players who could eventually play professionally.

## 2) Recruiting & Scouting Workflow

**Q: How do you recruit players?**
A: I watch games, tournaments, and showcases. I track players across multiple age groups and communicate with professional contacts and college coaches.

## 3) Tools Used

**Q: What tools do you use?**
A: Email, Hudl, and spreadsheets where I keep evaluations and notes.

## 4) Pain Points

**Q: Biggest difficulty?**
A: Organization of scouting. I see many players and it becomes difficult to track development over time and remember evaluations.

**Q: What breaks down?**
A: Notes and follow-ups. I may see a player months later and not remember my previous evaluation.

## 5) Reaction to Platform Idea

**Q: Would a combined recruiting + scouting platform help?**
A: Yes. If I could store evaluations, clips, and communication in one place, it would make scouting much more efficient.
