## Problem Section

### The Recruiting Process Is Broken

College recruiting is outdated and inefficient. Athletes spend hours sending cold emails to coaches who never respond. Coaches are overwhelmed by hundreds of emails per day and can't find the right talent. Important connections are missed because the tools are fragmented and disorganized.

- Athletes don't know if coaches ever watch their film
- Coaches waste time scrolling through emails and spreadsheets
- Communication is scattered across email, text, DMs, and phone calls
- Talented players get overlooked because they don't have the right connections

---

## Solution Section

### One Platform for Everything

Recruitr brings athletes and coaches together in one centralized platform. Athletes create a comprehensive profile with their bio, stats, academics, and highlight reels. Coaches search, filter, and connect with the right prospects in minutes instead of hours.

**For Athletes:**
- Build a complete recruiting profile in one place
- Upload highlights and full game film
- Get discovered by verified college coaches
- Track your recruiting journey

**For Coaches:**
- Search and filter athletes by sport, position, grad year, GPA, and region
- Watch highlights directly on athlete profiles
- Message prospects directly through the platform
- Build and manage your recruiting shortlist
