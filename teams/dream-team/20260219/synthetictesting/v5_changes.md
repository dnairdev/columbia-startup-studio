# Changes from V4

- **Section:** Demo labels
  **Change:** Added explicit "Coach Mode" toggle label and "Coach demo" text above the demo video.
  **Why:** V4 recommendation to make it clearer the demo shows the coach-side experience. Small UI copy addition to set expectations before the video plays.

- **Section:** CTA phrasing
  **Change:** Moved the email input prompt from "Enter your email to get early access" to "Enter your email to start recruiting for free" with an "↵ Enter" action hint.
  **Why:** "Start recruiting for free" is more action-oriented than "get early access." The enter-key hint reduces friction — makes it feel like one quick action rather than a form submission. Minor UX copy tweak.

- **Section:** Overall copy
  **Change:** No structural changes. Light tightening of punctuation and spacing. Added em dash formatting for consistency.
  **Why:** V4 crossed the 50% conversion threshold and qualitative feedback has converged. The same lines keep scoring highest across rounds. Major changes risk regression at this point. This is a polish pass, not a rewrite.

- **Section:** Branding
  **Change:** Added "R" logo mark and "Recruitr" wordmark to the top navigation area.
  **Why:** Several personas in earlier rounds mentioned the page felt unbranded. Adding minimal brand identity at the top anchors the experience without adding copy weight.
