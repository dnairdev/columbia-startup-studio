## Key Qualitative Themes

### What Improved

1. **Coach value tagline fix worked (19 mentions as strongest line):** "Video-first profiles coaches can actually evaluate in seconds" was the second most-cited line after "LinkedIn meets TikTok." Coach personas specifically said it matched the rest of the page's tone. "Now the whole page feels like one voice."

2. **Sports Parents bucket is highly reachable:** 67% resonance, 50% conversion, 8% dealbreaker in their first round. Parents connected with the simplification angle — "I've been managing my son's recruiting spreadsheet for 6 months, this would save me so much time." Strong signal that parents are a viable GTM segment.

3. **Conversion crossed 50% overall.** The stopping criterion threshold has been met. Conversion confidence at 52% agree+ is a meaningful milestone, driven primarily by transfer athletes, D2/D3 coaches, and the new parents bucket.

4. **Dealbreakers collapsed to 12%.** Down from 38% in V1. The remaining dealbreakers are concentrated in club coaches/scouts (25%) — primarily due to concerns about whether the platform covers sports beyond soccer.

### What Still Feels Off

1. **Club coaches/scouts remain the weakest returning bucket (25% dealbreaker):** Their concern is scope — "Does this work for basketball? Track? Or just soccer?" The copy says "sports" but the demo video shows soccer. There's a mismatch between the broad language and the narrow demo.

2. **"Phase 1 MVP" still polarizing (5 mentions):** Split reaction — some personas read it as refreshingly honest, others still see it as a red flag. Not a top concern anymore but still surfaces.

3. **No proof point (6 mentions):** Fewer personas brought it up than V3 (9 mentions), suggesting the demo video partially filled the trust gap. But coach personas in particular still want to see a number — "even 50 athletes would make me feel like this is real."

### Strongest Lines

- "The LinkedIn of sports meets TikTok" — 24 mentions
- "Recruiting, reimagined: video-first profiles coaches can actually evaluate in seconds" — 19 mentions
- "Recruiting, simplified" — 15 mentions
- "feels like social media — because that's where the talent already is" — 11 mentions

### Top Objections

- "Is this only for soccer?" (8 mentions, mostly club coaches)
- "No traction numbers or social proof" (6 mentions)
- "Phase 1 MVP label" (5 mentions)
- "How do I know coaches will actually use this?" (4 mentions)

## Recommendations for V5

1. **Minor copy polish only.** The page has crossed the 50% conversion threshold. Major structural changes risk regression. Focus on tightening language, not restructuring.
2. **Keep "Phase 1 MVP" label.** The negative feedback has dropped from 7 to 5 mentions and it reinforces the early-access positioning that drives email signups.
3. **Consider adding a "Coach demo" label** next to the video to make it even clearer what the demo shows.
4. **V5 should be the final version** unless testing reveals a regression. The copy has converged — qualitative themes are repeating and the same lines keep scoring highest.
