# Product Roadmap
 
## Week 1 - March 30
**Finalize Design & Soft Launch**
 
- Lock in the core product design (no more major pivots after this week)
- Polish the UI/UX for a presentable first impression
- Share with friends and close contacts for initial reactions
- Post on relevant Reddit communities (identify 2–3 target subreddits)
- Set up a lightweight feedback channel (form, DMs, or Discord)
 
## Week 2 - April 6
**Iterate on Real Feedback**
 
- Review feedback from early users and identify the top pain points
- Prioritize and ship quick fixes and high-impact improvements
- Follow up with early users to keep them engaged
- Track which features people actually use vs. ignore
 
## Week 3 - April 13
**Expand Reach**
 
- Broaden promotion beyond the initial circle
- Post in additional communities, forums, or social channels
- Reach out to micro-influencers or niche creators if relevant (can give them for free in exchange for promotion)
- Aim to grow the active user base meaningfully this week
 
## Week 4 - April 20
**Feature Freeze & Prep for Pitch**
 
- No new features, stability and polish only
- Collect usage data, testimonials, and key metrics
- Compile everything into a compelling pitch narrative
- Deadline: pitch-ready materials by end of week
