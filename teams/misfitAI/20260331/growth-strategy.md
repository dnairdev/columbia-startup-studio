# Misfit AI — Early Acquisition Strategy (NYC)

## Overview
Misfit AI is targeting young people in New York with a highly personal, lifestyle-driven product (AI outfit planning based on schedule + weather). 

We will focus on:
- Instagram (primary channel)
- LinkedIn (secondary channel)

---

# 1. Acquisition Channels & Focus

## Primary Channel: Instagram
We will use Instagram as our core growth engine through:
1. Organic Posting (misfit account)
2. Street-style / interview reels
3. Direct messaging (DMs) to users and influencers

## Secondary Channel: LinkedIn
Used for:
- Product storytelling
- Building credibility
- Driving waitlist traffic

---

# 2. Instagram Strategy

## 2.1 Create Dedicated @misfit Account

### Goal
Build a strong, aesthetic, NYC-focused brand presence.

### Content Types
- Outfit examples (AI-generated looks)
- “Day in NYC → outfit generated” posts
- Relatable pain points:
  - “15 mins wasted every morning deciding outfits”
- Before/after:
  - “What you planned vs what you should wear”

### Posting Frequency
- 1–2 posts/day
- 3–5 stories/day

---

## 2.2 Street Interviews + Reels

### Goal
Create engaging, viral, and highly relatable content.

### Format Ideas
- “What are you wearing today and why?”
- “How long did it take you to pick your outfit?”
- “Rate your outfit vs what AI would suggest”

### Execution
- Record in NYC (SoHo, West Village, campus areas)
- Keep clips short, fast-paced, aesthetic

### Volume
- 5–10 interviews per session
- 3–5 reels posted per week

---

## 2.3 Instagram DM Outreach (Users)

### Target Audience
- NYC-based
- Ages 18–28
- Posts outfits, lifestyle, GRWM, or aesthetic content

### Message Script (Primary)

hey! random but i’m building a small AI that plans outfits based on your schedule + weather (for NYC specifically)

i’m trying to make it actually useful lol — could i show you and get your thoughts?

---

### Volume
- ~10 DMs/day

### Expected Results
- 25% reply rate

---

## 2.4 Instagram DM Outreach (Influencers)

### Target
- NYC creators with 5k–50k followers
- Fashion, GRWM, lifestyle niches

### Message Script

hey! love your content — especially how you style outfits for different days

i’m building an AI that generates outfits based on your schedule + weather (NYC specific)

would love to give you early access + build something that actually fits your style

---

### Offer
- Early access
- Input into product direction
- Future collaboration opportunities

### Volume
- 3 influencers/day
- Goal: onboard 5–10 strong early creators

---

# 3. LinkedIn Strategy

## Goal
Establish credibility + attract thoughtful early adopters

## Content Themes

### 1. Building in Public
- “Why I’m building misfit AI”
- “The problem: decision fatigue in daily outfits”

### 2. Insights from Users
- “I talked to 30 people in NYC — here’s what I learned”
- Key pain points and patterns

### 3. Product Demos
- Short clips or screenshots
- “Input: schedule + weather → output: outfit”

### 4. Personal Story
- Why this problem matters to you
- NYC-specific context

---

## Posting Frequency
- 1 post/week

---

## Call to Action (CTA)

If you’re in NYC and want early access, drop a comment or DM me — looking for a small group of early users.

---

# 4. Success Metrics

## Instagram

### DMs
- Reply rate: >25%
- Conversion rate: >10%

### Content
- Saves > Likes (indicates real value)
- Comments like:
  - “I need this”
  - “This is literally my problem”

---

## Influencers
- Engagement quality (not just likes)
- Waitlist spikes after posts
- Comments asking how to access product

---

## LinkedIn
- Comments > likes
- Inbound DMs
- Waitlist signups from posts

---

# 5. How We Know a Channel is Working

A channel is working if:

### 1. Pull > Push
- Users ask:
  - “When can I use this?”
  - “Is this live?”

### 2. Conversations Get Easier
- Less convincing needed over time

### 3. Organic Growth Begins
- Referrals:
  - “My friend told me about this”
- Inbound interest increases
