# Weekly Update — Roam

**Date:** April 21, 2026

## 1. What we built last week

We added two new buttons to the app: a **Collections** button and a **Friends** button.

- **Friends button:** Gives users easier access to adding and inviting friends.
- **Collections button:** Lets users quickly find the collection/group they want to add a pin to without endless scrolling, and makes it easy to check what's inside a group without a bunch of extra clicks.

The goal was to cut down on friction in two of the most common user flows — inviting people and organizing pins.

## 2. What we're building this week

Mostly **scaling and polishing** based on the great feedback we got from our presentation last week.

We also learned about a competitor, **Tabi**, which has around 6k users. This week we're going to dig into what makes Roam different from Tabi and figure out how to sharpen that differentiation so we can lean into it.
