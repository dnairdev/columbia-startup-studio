## Four Questions to Answer

**1. What is your cold start type?**

Choose one: Network, Content/Supply, Marketplace, Group/Invite, AI/Algorithm, or none.

**2. Who is your hard side?**

Name specific people. Not demographics. "The 12 people in my dorm who..." not "college students." If your answer is a demographic, think harder. Density requires specificity.

**3. What are your two strategies?**

Use the decision table from class. Pick a primary strategy and a secondary strategy.

**4. What can you do THIS WEEK?**

One concrete action for each strategy. Not "think about it." Do it.
