## Part 1: Peer Review Learnings

What did you learn from today's peer review session?

- **What worked** when people used your product?
- **What confused** them or broke?
- **What's the biggest gap** in your product right now?
- **One improvement you shipped** based on what you heard. This should be a real change to your live product, not a plan. Include a link or describe what you changed.

## Part 2: Product Roadmap

This is the more important part. You have 8 weeks until Demo Day. What are you doing with them?

**What you need to learn about your product:**

These are product questions, not class topics. Think about things like: Who is our real audience? Is our onboarding clear? What makes the aha moment land? Is there a pain point that makes this valuable enough? Do people come back?

**What you need to build:**

Week-by-week breakdown of what you're building and who's doing it. The template has a table for this. Be specific. "Improve the app" is not a plan. "Add onboarding flow with 3-question survey" is.

**Demo Day vision:**

What does success look like on May 12? What story do you want to tell? What data do you need to support it?

**Biggest open question:**

The one thing your team is most unsure about right now. Write it down. We'll use these to shape the remaining classes.
