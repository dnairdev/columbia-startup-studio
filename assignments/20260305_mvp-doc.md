## What the MVP Doc Must Include

**1. Core flow**

The single path through your product: user → action → value. If you can't demo it in 5 minutes, you've scoped too much.

**2. Tech stack**

What you're building with. Languages, frameworks, tools, hosting.

**3. Team roles**

Who's building what. Who's on demand gen. Be specific — "everyone builds" is not a plan.

**4. What's faked**

What are you Wizard-of-Oz'ing or running as concierge? What's manual behind the scenes? This is not a negative — faking the hard parts is smart.

**5. Demand gen status**

What's currently running to drive traffic and signups. What are the numbers (visits, signups, conversion rate)?
